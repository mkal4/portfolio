SOME SHIT ABOUT MICHAEL

Michael Kalmin is a producer, writer, video editor, and content creator based in New York City.

After graduating Indiana University in December, 2014 with a Bachelor's degree in Arts Management, he started working for Anonymous Content, a leading production and management company. This led to his first job as a Production Assistant working on a music video for Madonna. Since then, Michael has expanded his background in entertainment and production by working on Believeland, an ESPN 30 for 30 documentary film about Cleveland sports, the Emmy award-winning show Mr. Robot on the USA Network, Marvel Television shows Daredevil and Luke Cage, a pilot for the new ABC drama Time After Time, documentaries for Vice Sports about NBA basketball players Jimmer Ferdette and Michael-Kidd Gilchrist, a segment for the Sports Illustrated website on the greatest moments in sports history, commercials for Verizon, Ford, Dove and Atlantic Broadband, and many other projects.

Michael has a solid background working both on set and in offices all over the tri-state area. He has recently worked as an assistant for the AICPA on a number of live webcasts and other projects, developing a keen sense of multimedia production while managing administrative responsibilities.

Michael enjoys creative writing and has experience in writing casting calls for MTV True Life. Michael has had his writing published on the official MTV website and Facebook page, targeting an audience of millions. Michael also has experience as a Research Assistant at the NYU Langone Medical Center, where he also volunteered, and as a volunteer for the Bellevue Medical Center. Michael's volunteer work has extended to soup kitchens around the world.
